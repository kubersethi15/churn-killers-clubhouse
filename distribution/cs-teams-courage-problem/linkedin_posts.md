Strategy: Clear numbered framework that explains the problem systematically. Easy to save and share.
============================================================

Three forces turned strategic advisors into professional excuse-makers:

1. The Support Team Mindset Migration
When companies moved support teams into CS, they imported the core principle: resolve immediate concerns with minimal escalation. Support optimizes for case closure. CS adopted this for conversation closure.

2. The Vendor-Customer Power Dynamic Myth
CS leaders teach that customers hold all the power in renewals. This creates a negotiating stance where CS argues from weakness instead of value.

3. The Metrics Misalignment
We measure customer satisfaction instead of customer progress. We celebrate CSMs who manage difficult conversations well instead of CSMs who eliminate the need for them.

The result: CS teams that predict churn perfectly but prevent it poorly.

The antidote: Courage-based conversations that focus on outcomes, not comfort.
