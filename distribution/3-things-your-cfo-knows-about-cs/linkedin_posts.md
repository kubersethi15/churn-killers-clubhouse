3 financial lenses your CFO uses to evaluate CS:

1. Unit Economics Reality
What's the fully-loaded cost to retain $1 of ARR? Include salary, benefits, tools, office space, management overhead, recruiting, training, and opportunity cost. Most CS teams think $0.15-0.25. Reality is $0.40-0.65.

2. Investment Payback Timeline
How long does CS headcount take to pay for itself? Not impact on retention rates, but when prevented churn revenue exceeds total cost of hire. CS leaders think quarterly. CFOs think 18-24 month payback periods.

3. Opportunity Cost Analysis
Every CS dollar is a dollar not spent on product, sales, or marketing. Would this CS investment generate more revenue as two AEs instead?

You see CS as essential. They see it as one option among many.
