Being the voice of the customer doesn't mean being the hands.

CS teams confuse relationship ownership with process ownership.

You can maintain customer intimacy while handing off:
- Contract negotiations to sales
- Bug reports to support
- Technical docs to product
- Training to professional services
- Security reviews to sales engineering

The customer doesn't care who fixes their problem. They care that it gets fixed fast by someone who knows what they're doing.

Your job as CS is to ensure the right person handles each issue with full context and appropriate urgency.

Orchestrate the experience. Don't own every piece of it.
