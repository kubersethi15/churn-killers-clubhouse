3 lies CS leaders tell themselves about renewals:

1. "We have visibility into renewal risk"
Reality: You see symptoms after decisions are made. Budget cuts happen in rooms you're not in. Champion changes happen before you know.

2. "Our action plans drive outcomes"
Reality: Most action plans address engagement, not business issues. 'More check-ins' doesn't solve 'budget got reallocated.'

3. "Historical data predicts future renewals"
Reality: Past usage patterns don't predict future business priorities. Yesterday's power user becomes tomorrow's budget cut.

The fix? Stop forecasting. Start influencing.

Get upstream of renewal decisions. Build relationships with procurement. Understand budget cycles.
