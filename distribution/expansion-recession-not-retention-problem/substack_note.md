Median SaaS NRR fell from 105% to 101% since 2021. Here is the part nobody says: gross retention barely moved. Customers are not leaving. They just stopped buying more. That is not a retention failure. It is an expansion recession, and if you accept a raw NRR target this planning cycle you just signed up to be blamed for it. https://churnisdead.com/newsletter/expansion-recession-not-retention-problem
